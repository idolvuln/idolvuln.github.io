About Medal Events

How to Play

Event Summary (Co-Op Guild Event)

In medal events, players can earn medals as they clear each verse. These medals can then be traded in at the medal exchange for a variety of rewards.


Verses for each event after verse 2 will unlock after the previous verse has been cleared. Some verses may require a certain amount of points to unlock regardless if the previous verse has been cleared.

Note: New verses cannot be unlocked by way of co-op battle.

Players can earn medals for individual completion rewards and, if they are a member of a guild, guild completion rewards.

Regarding Medals

Please see Event Notice for information regarding medals including acqusition, expiry, and limits.

Individual Completion Rewards

- Players can earn individual completion rewards when a certain amount of medals are collected.
Note: Player do not have to join a guild to earn individual completion rewards.

Guild Completion Rewards

- Players must join a guild to receive guild completion rewards.
- The amount of medals collected while in a guild will be added to the earned medals for that guild.
- Players must first individually earn 10,000 medals for their guild to be eligible for guild completion rewards. Once eligible, all guild completion rewards awarded to their guild up until that point will be sent all together.
- The total amount of medals earned for the guild will not be affected whether members join or leave the guild.
- Medals earned for your guild cannot be transferred to another guild.
- If a player changes guilds during the event period, they must again individually earn 10,000 medals for their new guild to be eligible for guild completion rewards. Again, once eligible, all guild completion rewards awarded to their new guild up until that point will be sent all together, excluding duplicates.
- Duplicate guild completion rewards cannot be earned regardless of how many times a player changes guilds.
