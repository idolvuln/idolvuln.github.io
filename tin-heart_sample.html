Medal Event: Tin Heart Notice

Thank you for playing SINoALICE.

The medal event, Tin Heart is now available.

Event Period

2020.09.10 04:00 to 2020.09.17.00:59

The event period is subject to change.


Event Summary

Defeat the nightmares appearing in event verses and collect tin medals.

These medals can be traded in a tthe event's medal exchange for rewards.
Plus, the more medals you collect will earn you completion rewards.
Utilize your resources and your endeavors will be rewarded bountifully.

Regarding Tin Medals

Tin Medals can be acquired in the following ways:
-Story participation rewards in the event verses
-Drop rewards in the event verses

Note: There are no special medal weapons for this event.

Tin Medals earned from this event will expire on 2020.09.24 01:59.
Note: Any medals remaining will be lost after the expiry date.

The max amount of medals a player can hold is 999,999,999. Any medals earned beyond that will be lost.


Event Rewards

Completion Rewards

Players will be awarded completion rewards based on the number of tin medals earned during the event. Further details regarding rewards and completion requirements can be checked on the special event page.

Items at the Medal Exchange

Tin medals can be traded at the medal exchange for items such as twilight crystals, nightmares, and other rewards.

Images here

The period players can trade tin medals at the medal exchange will end a the same time these medals expire.


Event Playguide

Recommended Character

Skills on specialty weapons will have their effects increased when you equip Pinocchio during this event. Plus, a special story can be read while using this character.


Additional Information

This event may appear again at a later date as a redux event. Medals obtained from this event cannot be used in a later event.
